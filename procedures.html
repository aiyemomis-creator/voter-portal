How to Vote

Your vote is your power. This guide walks you through everything you need to know — from registration to casting your ballot.

How to Vote — Step by Step

Follow these four simple steps to make your voice count on election day.

1

Register to Vote

Visit your local INEC office or use the online portal to register as a voter. Ensure your details are accurate and up to date.

2

Collect Your PVC

After registration, collect your Permanent Voter's Card (PVC) from the designated INEC office. This is your ticket to vote.

3

Find Your Polling Station

Locate your assigned polling unit ahead of election day. You can check online or at your local INEC office.

4

Cast Your Vote

On election day, arrive early, verify your identity with biometrics, collect your ballot paper, and vote in the private booth.
